Our Features

STOIC is designed for simplicity, scalability, and speed. All skillsets are welcome. No dataset is too large. And all features are real-time.

Data Import

You can import data from CSV files, databases like Oracle or SQL Server, and datastores like Greenplum or HDFS. Depending on the datasource, imports can be as fast as 100GB/s.

With our spreadsheet user interface, you can prepare your data with the necessary cleansings, mappings, and transformations, while interacting with the entire dataset in real-time, terabytes at your fingertips.

And when you are done, data is instantly available for deep analytics. Better yet, your data import script is automatically available for future imports of similar datasets.

Pivot Table

You can drill-down through multi-terabyte datasets with our pivot table. Simply drag and drop data fields onto the pivot, then specify the aggregations you need, like SUM or AVERAGE.

Using the familiar syntax of Excel® formulas, you can also define row filtering rules, or join columns across multiple sheets. And you can do all that without having to learn SQL.

Our pivot engine is fast, really fast: you can pivot a terabyte of data across 10 dimensions in less than a second. And it scales linearly with the size of your dataset and the number of pivot dimensions.

Pivot Charts

You can generate charts and maps directly from the pivot, without any complex range selection or column binding. Simply pivot your dataset, select your desired chart, and voilà!

We provide an extensive collection of charts that can be combined into dashboards and infographics. You can also use the standard D3.js library to add your own custom visualizations.

From there, charts and dashboards can be pushed to mobile devices like smartphones and tablets, without having to keep any cube data in memory. And you can export your pivot data to visualization tools like Tableau®.

Deep Analytics

You can further analyze your data with advanced machine learning and statistical techniques. STOIC lets you spot outliers, identify clusters, make predictions, and generate recommendations in just a few clicks.

Through our intuitive user interface, we make these advanced big data techniques accessible to regular users who are neither data scientists nor software engineers. If you can use Excel®, you can use STOIC.

And thanks to our unique in-memory data structures and computation engines, we make these calculations extremely fast, even for multi-terabyte datasets. In most cases, requests are processed within 1 second or less.
